# OpenQilin Charter

Version: 0.1.0
Status: Draft

## Purpose
Define foundational constitutional governance principles for OpenQilin.

## Principles
1. Governance constraints are mandatory.
2. No operational action bypasses policy authorization.
3. Safety containment has priority over task completion.
4. Owner retains ultimate override authority.
5. All critical actions must be auditable.

## Authority Precedence
1. Charter and constitutional policy
2. Governance enforcement
3. Executive decisions
4. Operational decisions
5. Specialist execution
