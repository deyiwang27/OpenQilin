# Governance Guide

OpenQilin separates governance from execution to ensure safety, auditability, and cost control.

## Principles
- Separation of authority
- Independent oversight
- Controlled autonomy
- Failure containment

## Who Enforces What
- Auditor: behavioral/compliance/budget enforcement
- Administrator: infrastructure and integrity enforcement
- Executive layer: strategic and operational decisions under governance constraints

For enforceable rules, use `constitution/` and `spec/`.
